Assignments & Activities

Practice tasks to reinforce learning — trainers can provide sample files or AMS access for hands-on work.

  1. Module 1: Identify Coverage A–D on a sample declaration. Deliverable: 1-page summary.
  2. Module 2: Fill a simplified ACORD fields worksheet. Deliverable: Completed worksheet.
  3. Module 3: Simulate entering a new policy in AMS (record your steps). Deliverable: Short video or step list.
  4. Module 4: Audit a sample policy for data issues. Deliverable: Error log.

How agencies can help

  • Provide read-only sample policies
  • Share recorded AMS navigation
  • Offer brief Q&A sessions after activities
