Assignments & Activities

Each module has a hands-on assignment. Trainers can provide sample files and AMS recordings for practice.

  1. Day 1: Identify Coverage A–D on a sample declaration. Deliverable: 1-page summary.
  2. Day 2: Create a short policy cycle diagram. Deliverable: Image or PDF.
  3. Day 3: Label components of an insurance document. Deliverable: Annotated screenshot.
  4. Day 4: Mock CSR calls (recording). Deliverable: Short audio/video clip.
  5. Day 5: AMS walkthrough checklist. Deliverable: Steps log.
  6. Day 6–11: Quoting & declaration analysis activities. Deliverable: Quote summary & screenshots.
  7. Day 12: ACORD fields worksheet. Deliverable: Completed worksheet.
  8. Day 13: SOP draft. Deliverable: SOP PDF.
  9. Day 14: Final reflection & certificate request.
