About LAVA Classroom

LAVA Classroom empowers Virtual Assistants with insurance knowledge and hands-on agency operations training. We focus on practical learning and system mastery.

Who is this for?

New Virtual Assistants, junior agents, and support staff who assist with client policies, renewals, and endorsements.

Learning Outcomes

  • Explain key Home Insurance coverages (A–D).
  • Identify common endorsements and ACORD forms.
  • Perform AMS and CRM support tasks accurately.
