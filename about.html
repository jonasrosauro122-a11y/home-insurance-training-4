Customer Success Leadership

The Customer Success Director (CSD) is responsible for providing strategic leadership, final decision authority, and cross-departmental alignment for Customer Success. This role establishes standards, policies, and overall direction while serving as the primary escalation point. Partners closely with Sales, IT, Finance, and Leadership, oversees client risk management and approves final decisions impacting accounts.

Account Management

The Account Manager is the primary client contact, managing relationships post-onboarding, conducting check-ins and reviews, coordinating training requests, identifying risks or growth opportunities, and communicating client needs to internal teams.
