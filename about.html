About this training

This portal was created to help Virtual Assistants and agency support staff gain practical knowledge in Home Insurance — policy structure, endorsements, and daily agency workflows.

Who it's for

New VAs, junior agents, and support staff who handle policy documents, endorsements, or billing tasks.

Learning outcomes

  • Explain Coverages A–D
  • Recognize common endorsements and ACORD forms
  • Complete simple policy data tasks in an AMS

Trainer tips

  • Use sample policies for hands-on practice
  • Record AMS navigation for trainees
  • Encourage trainees to ask questions live
