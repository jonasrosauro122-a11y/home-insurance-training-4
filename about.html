Customer Success Director (CSD)

Primary Objective: Provide strategic leadership, final decision authority, and cross-departmental alignment for Customer Success.
Core Responsibilities:
  • Establish Customer Success standards, policies, and direction
  • Serve as the escalation point for client and internal matters
  • Partner with Sales, IT, Finance, and Leadership on complex issues
  • Oversee client risk management and retention strategy
  • Approve final decisions impacting client accounts
  • The Customer Success Director and the client hold final decision authority for Level 2 changes

Account Management (AM)

Primary Objective: Serve as the primary client point of contact and support the ongoing alignment, stability, and growth of assigned accounts.
Core Responsibilities:
  • Manage client relationships post-onboarding
  • Conduct regular check-ins and account reviews
  • Coordinate training requests and service support
  • Identify risks, gaps, or growth opportunities
  • Communicate client needs to internal teams as appropriate
  • Account Managers escalate matters based on impact, complexity, or policy considerations

Executive Assistants (EA)

Primary Objective: Provide structured administrative and coordination support to the CSD and AMs to ensure clear communication, organized scheduling, and consistent documentation across client accounts.
Core Responsibilities:
  • Arrange and manage schedules for client and internal meetings
  • Organize, monitor, and support email communications on behalf of assigned partners
  • Attend client check-in meetings and onboarding meetings to support continuity and follow-up
  • Create and maintain documentation related to meetings, requests, and action items
  • Coordinate internal requests and follow-ups across departments based on direction from their working partner
  • The EA operates in a support and coordination capacity, ensuring information is documented, communicated, and routed appropriately while maintaining alignment with established processes and escalation paths

Trainers

Primary Objective: Deliver structured, client-requested training aligned with Lava standards and approved training menus.
Core Responsibilities:
  • Conduct VA training sessions onsite and online
  • Deliver system, carrier, and insurance concept training
  • Support skill development and readiness
  • Coordinate training delivery with Account Managers and Team Leads
  • Document training completion and outcomes
  • Training delivery aligns with the approved Lava training menu, including carriers, systems, AMS platforms, raters, insurance fundamentals, and ACORD forms

Team Leads (TL)

Primary Objective: Provide guidance, oversight, and consistency to VAs assigned under their team. They serve as the main point of contact for VA and AM communications.
Core Responsibilities:
  • Conduct daily, weekly, and monthly check-in meetings with VAs
  • Attend Client check-in meetings with AMs
  • Support AMs with escalations and decision support
  • Review account health and performance trends
  • Coordinate with Trainers to address skill gaps or training needs
  • Ensure processes and standards are followed consistently
  • Escalate issues to Customer Success Leadership when appropriate
  • The TL serves as the first formal VA escalation point within Customer Success
