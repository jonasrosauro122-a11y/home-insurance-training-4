About LAVA Classroom

LAVA Classroom was created to upskill Virtual Assistants and support teams in practical Home Insurance tasks — from policy understanding to AMS support.

Who is this for?

New VAs, junior agents, and support staff who handle policy documents, endorsements, and client communications.

Learning outcomes

  • Explain Coverages A–D
  • Identify common endorsements and ACORD forms
  • Complete key AMS tasks and support renewals

Trainer notes

  • Use sample policies for hands-on practice
  • Record AMS navigation for trainee review
  • Provide short quizzes after modules
