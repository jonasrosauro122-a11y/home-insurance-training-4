▶ Overview – Directors & Account Management

Customer Success Team Overview

The Customer Success Team manages client relationships post-sale and ensures services align with Lava standards and SOC-aligned practices.

Customer Success Director (CSD)

Provides strategic leadership, escalation management, and cross-department alignment.

Account Management (AM)

Primary client contact, conducts reviews, manages stability and growth of assigned accounts.

▶ Overview – Executive Assistants

Executive Assistants (EA)

Provide structured administrative and coordination support to CSD and AMs.

▶ Overview – Trainers

Trainers

Deliver structured client-requested training aligned with Lava standards.

Team Leaders

Provide oversight, conduct check-ins, and act as first VA escalation point.
