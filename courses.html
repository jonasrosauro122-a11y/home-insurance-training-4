14-Day Training Plan

Tap or click a day to see the full lesson breakdown.

Day 1 — Getting to Know You & Roles of a LAVA VA

What to Expect:

  • Welcome and introductions with team and trainer
  • Overview of training goals and LAVA culture
  • Understanding different roles of a LAVA Virtual Assistant
  • Overview of administrative tasks

What You'll Learn:

  • Expectations as a VA
  • Daily workflow and collaboration process
  • Basic communication and documentation practices

Day 2 — Insurance 101 & Policy Cycle

What to Expect:

  • Introduction to the world of insurance
  • Walkthrough of the policy life cycle
  • Learn basic terms: underwriting, binding, renewal, and cancellation

What You'll Learn:

  • Insurance process from start to finish
  • Client and agency interactions
  • Core concepts of policy management

Day 3 — Insurance Documents

What to Expect:

  • Explore common insurance forms and declarations
  • Understand the structure and types of documents

What You'll Learn:

  • Identifying policy documents correctly
  • Key details on certificates, forms, and endorsements

Day 4 — English 101 & Mock Calls

What to Expect:

  • Interactive English communication sessions
  • Basic CSR mock calls

What You'll Learn:

  • Enhance listening and speaking skills
  • Build confidence in handling real client calls

Day 5 — Introduction to AMS & CRM

What to Expect:

  • Explore agency management systems (AMS)
  • Learn CRM basics and data entry

What You'll Learn:

  • Navigation and data organization
  • Client information management

Day 6 — Raters & Carriers

What to Expect:

  • Overview of insurance raters and carrier systems
  • Understand how quoting platforms work

What You'll Learn:

  • Common carriers and rating tools
  • Quote submission and retrieval process

Day 7 — Home Insurance Types

What to Expect:

  • Learn different types of home insurance policies
  • Understand coverages and construction details

What You'll Learn:

  • Coverage forms (HO3, HO5, etc.)
  • Quoting workflow and inspection factors

Day 8 — Auto Insurance Introduction

What to Expect:

  • Auto coverage overview and key endorsements
  • Hands-on quoting practice

What You'll Learn:

  • Coverage breakdown and limits
  • Quoting process using carrier platforms

Day 9 — Trailer, Boat, RV & Umbrella

What to Expect:

  • Introduction to specialty lines
  • Understanding quoting requirements for each

What You'll Learn:

  • Coverages for recreational vehicles and watercraft
  • How umbrella policies work

Day 10 — Home Declaration Page Activity

What to Expect:

  • Hands-on analysis of home declarations
  • Simulated quoting session

What You'll Learn:

  • How to interpret policy data
  • How to generate and compare quotes

Day 11 — Auto Declaration Page Activity

What to Expect:

  • Hands-on analysis of auto declarations
  • Simulated quoting and verification

What You'll Learn:

  • Decoding auto policy details
  • Adjusting coverages based on client needs

Day 12 — ACORD Forms Overview

What to Expect:

  • Understand ACORD forms for personal and commercial use
  • Highlight key data fields and accuracy tips

What You'll Learn:

  • How to complete ACORD forms
  • Identifying crucial information for submission

Day 13 — SOP Lecture & Final Exam

What to Expect:

  • Review of Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)
  • Writing practice and preparation for exam

What You'll Learn:

  • Documentation and process consistency
  • Assessment of all topics covered

Day 14 — Recognition & Certificates

What to Expect:

  • Feedback and reflection session
  • Presentation of certificates

What You'll Learn:

  • Celebrating completion and next steps
  • Review of learning journey and performance
