Training Course Overview

The Home Insurance Training course covers core areas that every insurance VA or agent support staff should master. Below are the main modules included:

Module Topic Learning Focus
Module 1 Introduction to Home Insurance Understand policy structure, coverages A–D, and basic terms.
Module 2 Policy Documentation Learn about ACORD forms, endorsements, and declarations.
Module 3 Processing & Support Handling renewals, endorsements, and billing inquiries efficiently.
Module 4 Quality and Compliance Ensuring data accuracy and adherence to agency standards.
